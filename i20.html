Flight Deck

Piloting and Navigation

Flight controls occupy only two cubic meters ensuirng clear communication. The piloting system for the i20 plugs directly into the pilot’s brain (NB pilot must come from a species with a brain). Think about a vector and you’re on your way.

The Navitron recognizes 5000 different hand-motions. With a snap of your fingers, you can auto-plot a course. Then pinch, swipe, mash, cradle, and chop your way to a plan that optimizes for all of your parameters.

"It's great having a friend next to you at all times." - an outgoing navigator

Life Support

Food, medicine, air, and water recycling

The i20’s integrated life support systems allow one person to support the needs of your entire crew. Controls for air systems, water recycling, medicine distribution, surgery, and cooking have all been integrated.

“Our last life support office was an octodroid. Being able to smell probably would have helped both the food and air quality a lot.” - a hungry pilot

Communications

Long distance and onboard comms

Our state-of-the-art communications system allows for both inter- and intra- ship communications. Text, voice, and video messages can be sent anywhere. Whether you’re cruising through hyperspace or careening through an asteroid belt, keeping in touch with your crew has never been easier.

“Ever since the discovery of the “Babel Fish,” communications has been less about learning languages and more about understanding cultures.” - an anthropologist (probably)

Mech Tech

Technology that will solve all of your problems

Your i20 comes equipped with over 200 drones providing you with remote sensing capabilities and tools for working on your hull. There are also robotic and mechanical systems throughout the ship that need to be well oiled in order for your ship to run like a well-oiled machine.
